Rochell Hatfield

I am a proud Native American from the Navajo Tribe. I was raised in Kent, WA and I now reside in Bothell, WA. Even though I will forever live in the Pacific Northwest, I love going back to Arizona to soak up the sun.

I am a mother, a hiker, and a professional. I have an adventurous spirit that takes me to new heights, whether summiting The Matterhorn in Eastern Oregonâ€™s Wallowas or carrying my daughter to her first alpine lake. I love the outdoors, having new experiences, and bringing my family along for the ride.

I've already climbed to the top of the mountains, but I won't stop there...

It took me a while to realize what I wanted to do with my life. For a couple of years, I've been struggling to figure out what I wanted to major in. I went from aspiring to become a journalist, an English literature professor, and then to business/ marketing. It wasn't because I was doing poorly in the classes-I excelled- but my heart wasn't in it. I had a goal of one day opening my own business and using my knowledge of psychology, sociology, and micro-economics. My issue was that I was wasting my creative, artistic talent.

My passion is creating beautiful things that will be remembered beyond my life. My adoration is to learning new things through research, podcasts, videos, and books.

It wasn't until Eastern Washington University's Visual Communication Design Department that I realized I wanted to help businesses succeed by being a UX Designer/ Product Designer.

I live a design lifestyle with my Bachelors in Design and a UX/UI Certificate.
